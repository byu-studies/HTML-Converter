His Body Breaks

His body breaks

long before he

hangs on the cross.

He feels it in

the slow drag

of sickness

picked up

from crowds,

in the joints

worn thin

from long

walks, the

strain of

forty-day

fasts.

He is held

together with

God and glue

by Golgotha.

Eloi eloi,

he allows

himself at last,

lama

sabachthani?

—James Goldberg
