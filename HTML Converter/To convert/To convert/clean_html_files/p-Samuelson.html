She Believed

She believed her offering was enough,

And it was.

More than enough.

She believed she had in her hands

Enough to feed five thousand,

And there was.

She believed

That what her Savior was in search of

Was her.

She was the one who gave

Just what He needed

To be able to teach

And to save.

She believed

And didn’t doubt.

She had courage

To propose

Amidst the scoffs

And hunger throes—

A brave girl

Believed.

—Sarah Richards Samuelson
